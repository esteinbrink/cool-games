A Cool Game That I Like: Disney Dreamlight Valley

Get back to your childhood by working with Mickey Mouse and friends to restore Dreamlight Valley!

About

Disney Dreamlight Valley is a life simulation game where you, as the player, work to restore Dreamlight Valley and fight a mysterious plight known as "the Forgetting."


Disney Dreamlight Valley was developed by Gameloft. In the game, you can develop friendships with classic Disney characters, like Mickey, Minnie, Goofy, and Donald Duck. In the game, you can farm, fish, forage, cook, and mine minerals. It is available on the following platforms:

  • PC
  • Nintendo Switch
  • XBox One
  • XBox Series X/S
  • Playstation 4/5

Reasons I love it:

  • It's super low stress! There's quests to complete, but no time limits, and you can basically work on whatever makes you happy. If you just want to farm carrots, go for it! Love fishing? Do that all day! There's no one perfect way to play.
  • The nostalgia! Running around with Mickey Mouse at my side makes me feel like a kid again.
  • The customizability! To the left, you can see my avatar, which I've styled with clothes and a hairstyle that feel like they suit me.

Cooking: a Disney Dreamlight Valley Essential

In Disney Dreamlight Valley, cooking is an essential skill. You'll need to cook for quests, and you'll want to have a couple meals on you when you're out and about to charge up your energy. Friendship: an Essential Mechanic

In Disney Dreamlight Valley, "friendship" is one of the most important mechanics in the game, and it's important to know about from the beginning. When you reach level 2 friendship with a character, you'll be able to assign them a skill-either farming, foraging, fishing, digging, or mining.

When you hang out with a friend with a certain skill, you get extra friendship points with that character when you do something related to that skill. For example, I set Mickey Mouse's skill to farming, so when I hang out with him and harvest crops, he gets extra friendship points.

If you're doing anything in the game, take a friend with you! Preferably a friend with that specific skill, but taking any friend along while you do chores or catch fish will increase their friendship level, and increasing friendship levels unlocks things like new furniture, clothing, and more quests!
