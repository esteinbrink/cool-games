A Cool Game That I Like: Stardew Valley

A cozy farming sim for those of us who harbor pastoral fantasies but also like staying on our couches

About

Stardew Valley is a cozy life simulation game about abandoning your life in the city in order to go restore your grandfather's farm.


Stardew Valley was created by Eric Barone, who is known professionally as ConcernedApe. The game has a huge worldwide following, and can be played on many platforms, including:

  • PC
  • Nintendo Switch
  • XBox One
  • Playstation 4
  • iOS
  • Android

Reasons I love it:

  • It is so cozy! I love curling up on the couch to harvest some crops and pet my chickens. There is also a huge amount of variation in play styles - you can play completely chill and cozy, or you can set a goal for yourself to complete in a certain amount of in-game time.
  • The community! The Stardew Valley community is super supportive of new players and creates some of the best stuff. For examples, see:
    • The Stardew Valley Fan-made Wiki
    • The Stardew Valley Subreddit
    • The Stardew Valley Memes Subreddit
  • The developer! Stardew Valley has been around since 2016, and as of writing this (in February of 2023), ConcernedApe is still putting out patches for mobile versions of the game. ConcernedApe is obviously continuously committed to making the game better, and that is one of the most exciting parts about playing it.
